HISTORY

The city is the seat of Jönköping Municipality, which has a population of 143,579 (2022) and is Småland's most populous municipality.Jönköping is also the seat of Jönköping County which has a population of 367,064 (2022).
Jönköping is the seat of a district court and a court of appeal as well as the Swedish National Courts Administration.
It is the seat of the Swedish Board of Agriculture
